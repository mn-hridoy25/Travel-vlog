MY TRAVEL VLOG

A few days ago me and my friends came back from Sitakunda , a sightseeing place in Chittagong . In this vlog I am going to share that experience with you. Hope everyone will read carefully and share your opinion.

We had no preplans for the tour. After finishing classes in college, we were sitting at the tong shop and having tea. At that time, Anupam came and said that he was going somewhere. After some discussion it was decided that we all can go to Sitakunda together. But the condition is that we have to go today, due to which our trip became more exciting. From there I went to the station and bought the ticket. Our train is at 8:30 pm. So we came home early and packed everything and reached the station by 8 o'clock. The train arrived at 8:20. We all sat in our respective seats and the train started moving. Because of the night we could not see anything outside. So we started spending time by telling different kinds of stories. We got down at various stations in between and had some food. That's how time passed. Since dawn, the train has almost arrived very close to Chittagong. Then we could see many things outside. We were surprised to see many big mountains, rivers, different kinds of big factories. Then came to our desired station and got down.
